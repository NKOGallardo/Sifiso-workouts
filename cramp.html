How to Stop a Cramp

What to Do How it Helps Example
Stretch the muscle Relaxes the cramped muscle Pull toes toward head for calf cramp
Massage the muscle Increases blood flow and relaxes it Rub the cramped area gently but firmly
Apply heat Loosens tight muscles Use a warm towel or heating pad
Apply ice (after cramp) Reduces soreness after cramp Ice pack for 10-15 minutes
Drink water or electrolytes Rehydrates the body Drink water or a sports drink
Move gently Stops the muscle from stiffening Walk or shake the cramped limb lightly
